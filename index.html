Frequently Asked Questions

1. Account and Access

How do I create an account? +
You can create an account by clicking on the Register button on the homepage and filling in your college email and required details.
What if I forget my password? +
Use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page to reset your password securely.

2. Subjects and Notes

Where can I find notes for my subjects? +
Notes are organized by department and semester in the 'Notes' section. Browse and download them as needed.
Can I download the notes? +
Yes, most notes are available in PDF format for offline access.

3. Events and Workshops

How do I register for upcoming events? +
Visit the 'Events' tab, select the event you're interested in, and click 'Register' to participate.
Will I receive certificates? +
Certificates are usually awarded upon completion or participation in the event.

4. Announcements and Notifications

Where can I see the latest announcements? +
Announcements are posted on the homepage and in the 'Announcements' section for your convenience.

5. Discussion and Queries

How can I ask questions or clear doubts? +
Use the 'Ask a Query' form or participate in the discussion forum under your course section.

6. Technical Issues

The site is not loading. What can I do? +
Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. Contact support if the issue continues.

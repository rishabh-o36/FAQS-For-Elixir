Frequently Asked Questions

1. Account and Access

How do I sign up on the platform? +
Click the "Register" button on the homepage and fill in your college email and other required details.
I forgot my login credentials. What should I do? +
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.

2. Subjects and Notes

How do I find subject-wise notes? +
Go to the "Notes" section and choose your department and semester to view all available materials.
Are the notes downloadable? +
Yes, most notes are available in PDF format and can be downloaded for offline use.

3. Events and Workshops

How can I register for events? +
Visit the "Events" tab, select the event you're interested in, and click the "Register" button.
Are certificates provided? +
Yes, certificates are usually given for participation or completion, depending on the event.

4. Announcements and Notifications

Where can I see the latest announcements? +
Announcements are displayed on the homepage and under the "Announcements" section.

5. Discussion and Queries

How can I ask questions or clear doubts? +
You can use the “Ask a Query” form or post in the discussion forum under your course section.

6. Technical Issues

The site is not loading — what can I do? +
Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact support.
